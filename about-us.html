How We Work

This is how we work at metsli

  • Constant Improvement

    We like to go the extra mile for our customers, just as we do for our team mates. Many of us meet many of our clients' users in person. We do this to listen to your feedback - both good and bad - to share our vision and to help our customers see the people behind Metsli

  • Never Stop Learning

    Life doesn't stand still and neither do we. We read inspiring books together and attend conferences in order to grow and improve. "Wild experiment weeks" are another way we encourage everyone on the team to learn and try new things.

  • Strong Teamwork

    We are not a family - we are one hell of a team. We work for common goals and always have each others' backs. Every few months we travel to meet up somewhere in the world and spend quality time together. This keeps our ties strong and helps us see the human faces behind the Slack avatars. It also teaches us how to say hello in all the languages in the world.
